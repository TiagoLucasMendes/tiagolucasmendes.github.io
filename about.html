Leadership team

Our team is composed of highly qualified and dedicated professionals, whose work is fundamental to boost space exploration and advancing the limits of human knowledge.

Bill Nelson.

NASA Administrator

Bill Nelson is NASA's 14th administrator as of May 3, 2021, tasked with realizing the Biden-Harris administration's vision for the agency.

Palm Melroy

NASA Deputy Administrator

As deputy administrator, Melroy performs the functions and exercises the powers delegated by the administrator, assists the administrator in making final decisions of the agency and acts on behalf of the administrator in his absence, performing all functions necessary to govern NASA's operations.

Robert D. Cabana

NASA Associate Administrator

Former NASA astronaut, currently serving as the agency's associate administrator, its third highest-ranking executive, and highest-ranking civil servant. Prior to assuming this position on May 17, 2021, Cabana was director of the John F. Kennedy Space Center from NASA in Florida.

Susie Perez Quinn

NASA Chief of Staff

Susie Perez Quinn is the chief of staff at NASA, where she works with NASA Administrator Bill Nelson and senior staff to shape the strategic direction of the agency, while overseeing and articulating various policies and programs.

Bale Dalton

NASA Deputy Chief of Staff

Bale Dalton is NASA's deputy chief of staff, where he assists Administrator Bill Nelson in shaping the strategic direction and oversee the operations of one of America's most innovative agencies.

Dr. Bhavya Lal

Associate Administrator for Technology, Policy, and Strategy in the NASA Administration

Responsible for providing evidence-based advice to NASA's leadership on domestic and foreign policy issues, strategic planning and investments in technology. She also provides executive leadership and direction to the Office of Technology, Policy and Strategy (OTPS).
